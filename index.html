Welcome to When is my Exam!?

IoBM exam timetables made easy. Quick, painless search. Just enter your course code and instantly find out when your exam is! Enables you to easily share your course schedules with friends. Do you cower at the sight of those huge PDF files each time the hourlies or finals roll by? Well, this app is the answer to your prayers.
